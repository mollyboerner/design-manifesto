What Makes These Good?

Amount of White Space
The white space on these websites really emphasizes the art on their pages, which should be the main focus.
The Fonts
The simple fonts used increase legibility.
It's Condensed
Each page is condensed to the most important information. You have to click a link or an image to get more information.
